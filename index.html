Team Relay @ International Drone Day & Mini Airshow

May 7-8, 2016

Race format:

16 teams, 4 pilots per team, 64 pilots in total.
5 Rounds1 of 4 Heats, with 4 teams in each Heat.
Every team races against every other team exactly once over 5 rounds.2

Race duration: 10 minutes.
Race distance: 20 laps.
Relay format: only one pilot per team in the air at any time. Penalties apply for violating the rules.

1 Rounds reduced from 5 to 4 following a pilot vote due to delayed start.
2 Race duration reduced from 10 to 8 minutes after Round 2 to make up time.
